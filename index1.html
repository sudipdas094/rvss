ABOUT RVSS Group

Unifying IT and AV: Communications made easy

SERVICES

RVSS Managed Services provides highly trained and certified AV/IT professionals to act as an extension of your team. Our team will report directly to your office and will provide daily support in-room scheduling, setting up and running events, preventative maintenance, basic troubleshooting and break/fix work. As your needs change, our team will change to match your requirements. 

Our professional services ensure that you can rely on RVSS to provide complete turnkey solutions to your requirements; from initial needs analysis, consultancy, system and solution design, implementation, project management, training, and technology adoption.

RVSS's event production services include planning, technical design, logistics, audio, video, as well as lighting equipment set-up, technical staffing, recording, and post-production wrap up. Our expert team helps you deliver exceptional results for your audience and provides you with temporary staffing for all of your live events. Use our highly trained staff on a short term or a long-term basis to help you reduce your payroll costs and save you time in training and hiring.

RVSS Maintenance Services are there when you need them. Our team will be able to work with you via phone and email to do first-level troubleshooting. If we are unable to resolve the issue, we will work with you to schedule a RVSS technician to come onsite to do in-person troubleshooting and issue resolution. We can also work with you to provide in-depth preventative maintenance

Global Account Management has become a vital part of RVSS's offering to multinational businesses that have requirements in multiple geographic locations, and desire to have a unified deployment and managed services for their audiovisual needs.

We expanded our portfolio of digital workplace services to help you identify the best solutions for your workplace.
